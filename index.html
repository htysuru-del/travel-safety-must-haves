How To Pack It

The goal is a calm personal bag: documents first, comfort in reach, hygiene in one pouch.

Top pocket

Passport holder, travel wallet, phone charger, lip balm, gum, and sanitizer should be reachable without opening the whole bag.

Main compartment

Use the tote for headphones, neck pillow, water bottle, book, journal, organizer pouch, and a light snack pack.

Carry-on

Keep packing cubes, extra medicine, bandages, and backups in a tidy layer so you do not have to unpack at the gate.
