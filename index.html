How To Choose

Prioritize what you touch often.Passport holder, travel wallet, and anti-theft bag should be easy to open without spilling everything.
Keep hotel safety compact.The best safety items are small enough to actually pack every time.
Separate tracker and holder decisions.A tracker helps locate items. A holder makes the tracker easier to attach and harder to lose.
Use soft colors when possible.Blush, beige, taupe, and sage fit the Pinterest image while still being practical.
